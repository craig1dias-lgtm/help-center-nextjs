## Card Sizes and Pricing

Our custom FIFA-style cards come in three different sizes to suit your needs and preferences. Each size is carefully crafted to provide the best quality and visual impact.

### Standard Cards

Our Standard football cards come in three sizes:

- **Small:** 28cm x 18cm - Perfect for desks, shelves, or smaller spaces
- **Medium:** 39cm x 25cm - Our most popular size, ideal for walls and displays
- **Large:** 56cm x 37cm - Maximum impact for feature walls and special gifts

Standard football cards are brought to life on ultra strong PVC board. Giving you an awesome real-life football card with a glossy finish and excellent durability.

### Metal Cards

For a premium option, our Metal cards are available in the same three sizes:

- **Small:** 28cm x 18cm
- **Medium:** 39cm x 25cm
- **Large:** 56cm x 37cm

Metal cards are forged onto a 100% metal shield. Complete with a superb matte finish and a stunning true metal feel.

## Which Size Should You Choose?

Choosing the right size depends on where you plan to display your custom card:

- **Small (28cm x 18cm):** Ideal for desks, shelves, or smaller wall spaces. Perfect for gifts and personal collections.
- **Medium (39cm x 25cm):** Our most popular size. Great balance between impact and space efficiency. Looks fantastic on walls and in displays.
- **Large (56cm x 37cm):** Maximum visual impact. Perfect for feature walls, man caves, sports bars, or special occasions.
