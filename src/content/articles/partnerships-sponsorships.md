## Partnerships & Sponsorships

We're always looking to collaborate with like-minded organizations, content creators, and businesses. Our partnership and sponsorship programs offer mutually beneficial relationships that help both parties grow.

### Types of Partnerships We Offer

#### Content Creator Partnerships

We work with YouTubers, Instagram influencers, TikTok creators, and bloggers who create content related to:

- Football/soccer
- Sports collectibles
- Personalized gifts
- Sports memorabilia
- Gaming (especially FIFA/EA FC)

As a content partner, you'll receive:
- Custom affiliate links with tracking
- Special discount codes for your audience
- Commission on sales generated through your links
- Free products for review and giveaways
- Exclusive partnership deals for long-term collaborations

#### Business Partnerships

We collaborate with businesses in complementary industries:

- Sports merchandise retailers
- Football academies and clubs
- Event organizers
- Gift shops
- Corporate gift services

Business partnerships can include:
- Wholesale opportunities
- Co-branded products
- Cross-promotional marketing
- Bulk order discounts
- Custom product development

#### Sponsorships

We sponsor selected events, teams, and initiatives that align with our brand values:

- Youth football teams
- Charity football tournaments
- Sports-related community events
- Football content creators
- Football podcasts and shows

### Partnership Requirements

While we consider each partnership opportunity individually, we generally look for:

- Alignment with our brand values and target audience
- Established presence with engaged followers/customers
- Professional approach to collaboration
- Long-term relationship potential
- Mutual benefit and growth opportunities

### How to Apply for a Partnership

If you're interested in partnering with us, please send us the following information:

1. Your name and organization/channel name
2. Type of partnership you're interested in
3. Links to your website, social media profiles, or content
4. Brief description of your audience/customers
5. Your partnership ideas and goals
6. Any relevant metrics (followers, engagement rates, etc.)

Send your partnership inquiry to [partnerships@yourstore.com](mailto:partnerships@yourstore.com) with the subject line "Partnership Opportunity".
