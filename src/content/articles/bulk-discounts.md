## Bulk Discounts

We offer special pricing for bulk orders to make our custom FIFA-style cards even more affordable when ordering in larger quantities. Whether you're ordering for a team, an event, or as gifts, our bulk discounts can help you save.

### Bulk Discount Structure

Our bulk discount structure is designed to provide better value as your order quantity increases:

| Quantity | Discount |
|----------|----------|
| 3-5 cards | 10% off |
| 6-10 cards | 15% off |
| 11-20 cards | 20% off |
| 21-50 cards | 25% off |
| 51+ cards | Contact us for custom pricing |

### How to Order in Bulk

To place a bulk order:

1. **For orders of 3-50 cards:**
   - Add the desired number of cards to your cart
   - The discount will be automatically applied at checkout
   - Complete your order as normal

2. **For orders of 51+ cards:**
   - Contact our support team at [support@yourstore.com](mailto:support@yourstore.com)
   - Include "Bulk Order Inquiry" in the subject line
   - Provide details about your order (quantity, size preferences, timeframe)
   - We'll respond with a custom quote within 1-2 business days

### Bulk Order FAQs

#### Can I order different sizes in a bulk order?
Yes, you can mix and match sizes. The discount is applied based on the total number of cards.

#### How long does production take for bulk orders?
Production time depends on the quantity and complexity of your order:
- 3-20 cards: 5-7 business days
- 21-50 cards: 7-10 business days
- 51+ cards: To be discussed based on specific requirements

#### Can I make changes to individual cards after placing a bulk order?
Minor changes can be accommodated before production begins. Please contact our support team as soon as possible with any changes.

#### Do you offer design services for bulk orders?
Yes, we can provide design assistance for bulk orders. Additional fees may apply depending on the complexity of the design work required.

#### Is there a minimum order quantity for bulk discounts?
Yes, our bulk discounts start at orders of 3 or more cards.
